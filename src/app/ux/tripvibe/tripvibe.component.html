UX Design Research

Responsibilities

User Research: Competitive Analysis, User Interviews, Affinity Mapping, Mind Mapping, User Personas

UX Design: Sketches, Wireframing, Prototyping, Usability Testing

Tools Used

Figma, Adobe, ProtoPie, Miro, Maze, Zoom

Problem

How might we improve the process of trip planning for travelers?

Solution

Trip planning matching application:

Approach

1. Empathize: Qualitative Research - User Interviews, Competitive Analysis

2. Define: User Personas, Affinity Mapping

3. Ideate: Sketching, Wireframing, Mind Mapping

4. Prototype: Mid to high fidelity Prototypes

5. Test: Usability Testing

Empathize

Conducted 7 user interviews to understand usersâ€™ pain points and needs when planning trips and the user journey.

Analyzed the problem space and current state-of-the-art solutions.

Made design decisions based on current mental models and current innovative approaches to planning such as AI solutions and brought new ideas based on user feedback.

Define

Synthesized the collected data from the interviews into an affinity map to help form insights and possible ideas to solve the problems.

Based on the affinity map, User Personas were made to empathize better with the users:

The User Personas helped in making a mind map that highlights the pain points and the opportunities for improvement.

Ideate

Given these research findings, mind mapping helped in generating design ideas in the form of wireframes.

A moodboard was made in order to decide on typography, colors, images and logo that match the TripVibe ideas.

Prototype

The moodboard and the design ideas helped in reaching mid to high fidelity prototypes.

Test

To evaluate this prototype, performed usability testing with the participants.

This was followed by a quantitative survey that asked participants to describe their thought processes while performing the tasks.

Gathered mostly positive responses to the prototype but also paths to improvement, such as new ideas to add to the application and improving current designs in terms of accessibility.
